faq
do i need to do anything to get 2×? ▼
nope. the promotion applies automatically. you'll see higher limits outside peak hours without any changes to your account settings.
does bonus usage count against my weekly limit? ▼
no. the additional usage you get during off-peak hours doesn't count toward any weekly usage limits on your plan.
what happens when the promo ends? ▼
after march 28, usage limits return to standard levels at all hours. no change to your plan or billing.
does it apply on weekends? ▼
nope — the 2× promo is weekdays only. weekends use standard limits the whole time.
i'm on enterprise. do i get this? ▼
enterprise plans are excluded from this promotion. it applies to free, pro, max, and team plans only.
